# 2026-03-08

The buddy system from Thinking in Bets — Duke's point that you need a truthseeking group to counteract your own biases — is spot on, but she undersells how hard it is to actually find one. Building that group from scratch, recruiting people who genuinely want accuracy over comfort, is a real coordination problem.

That's one of the underrated perks of demanding jobs: the selection filter does the work for you. High-stakes environments attract people who can't afford to bullshit themselves, so the buddy system forms organically. You don't have to convince anyone that truthseeking matters — the job already did that.
