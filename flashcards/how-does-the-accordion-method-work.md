<summary>How does the "accordion method" for preparing talks work, and why prepare by speaking instead of writing?</summary>

Start at 3 minutes, compress to 2, 1, then 30 seconds — each round forces you to find the essential pillars. Then expand back up. The result: a talk that's internalized (not memorized), works at any length, and can't be derailed because you know the structure in your bones. No script to forget.

See: [The Accordion Method](../ideas/the-accordion-method.md)

</details>
