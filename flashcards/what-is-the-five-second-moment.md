<summary>What is the "five-second moment" and how does it change the way you build a story?</summary>

Every story worth telling is about a singular moment of transformation or realization that takes five seconds or less. The practical consequence: build from the end. Identify the moment of change first, then construct the beginning as its opposite. The story exists to bring that moment into focus.

See: [The Five-Second Moment](../ideas/the-five-second-moment.md)
