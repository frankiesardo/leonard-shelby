<summary>Why is "lowering the courage threshold" more impactful than demanding courage from people?</summary>

Courage is rarer than genius and is the true bottleneck for human output. Instead of asking for more courage, design systems that require less: trial runs, lower stakes, reversibility. Instagram's "trial reel" feature removes fear from creation; Shopify reduces the courage cost of starting a business. Every system that makes trying cheaper expands human potential.
