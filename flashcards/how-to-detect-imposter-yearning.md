<summary>How do you tell the difference between an authentic career yearning and an imposter yearning installed by someone else?</summary>

Play the Why Game: keep asking "why do I want this?" until you reach the root. If you find a chain of authentic reasoning built from your own experience, the yearning is real. If you trace it back to a moment when someone else installed it — a parent's expectation, society's script, a childhood dream you never re-examined — you've found an imposter. The key question: are your influences *information* you've evaluated and chosen, or are they *inside your brain masquerading as you*?

See: [Imposter Yearnings](../ideas/imposter-yearnings.md)
