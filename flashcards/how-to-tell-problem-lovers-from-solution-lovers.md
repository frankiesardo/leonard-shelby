<summary>How do you tell the difference between problem-lovers and solution-lovers in a team?</summary>

During stable times, they look exactly the same. The difference only surfaces when things change — platform shifts, reorgs, new technology. Problem-lovers adapt and get curious. Solution-lovers cling and defend. Tobi deliberately induces change partly to reveal which camp people are in.

See: [Fall in Love with Problems, Not Solutions](../ideas/fall-in-love-with-problems.md)
