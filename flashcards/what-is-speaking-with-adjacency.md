<summary>When explaining a product or idea through storytelling, why should you match theme instead of content?</summary>

Don't tell a story about your product — tell a story that shares the same theme, meaning, or message, then "snap" it into place. A scientist selling lab tubes tells a story about buying different apples for his picky family, then connects: "That's what my company does." The snap is powerful because the audience does the connecting themselves, making it stick.
