<summary>What is the Maturity Continuum and why does the sequence matter?</summary>

The three stages are dependence → independence → interdependence. Habits 1-3 build independence (Private Victory), Habits 4-6 build interdependence (Public Victory), Habit 7 sustains them all.

The sequence matters because interdependence is a choice only independent people can make. Dependent people can't genuinely collaborate — they're still looking to others for validation and direction. You have to master self-governance before you can effectively cooperate. Skipping independence produces codependence, not interdependence.
