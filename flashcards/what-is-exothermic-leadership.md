<details>
<summary>What does Tobi Lütke mean by "exothermic" leadership?</summary>

Companies at room temperature don't outperform. The best leaders inject heat into the system — energy, urgency, dissatisfaction with the status quo. The critical problem: the organizational immune system fights heat-injectors, trying to cool them down and normalize them. Founder-led companies stay innovative because founders can protect other exothermic people.
