<summary>Naval distinguishes "peace of mind" from "peace from mind." What's the difference, and why does it matter?</summary>

Peace *of* mind means a calm mind that thinks good thoughts. Peace *from* mind means freedom from the constant chatter altogether. Most happiness-seeking activities (sports, psychedelics, sex, even conversation) are indirect attempts to get peace from mind — to silence the monkey mind for a moment. The direct route is understanding: seeing your own thought patterns clearly enough that they naturally quiet down. Meditation is not a special activity — it's the practice of watching your mind 24/7.
