<summary>What is AVP (Acknowledge, Validate, Permit) and how does each step change your relationship to a difficult feeling?</summary>

**Acknowledge** = say hi to the feeling, which makes it a *part* of you instead of *all* of you (jealousy moves from the driver's seat to an annoying backseat passenger). **Validate** = tell yourself why the feeling makes sense — not agreeing with it, not endorsing behavior, just recognizing it as real ("I've been working hard and didn't get promoted, so jealousy makes sense"). **Permit** = give yourself explicit permission to have the feeling ("I allow myself to feel jealous"). Each step reduces the feeling's grip because feelings that are acknowledged, validated, and permitted don't need to shout to be heard.
