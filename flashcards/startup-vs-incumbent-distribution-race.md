<summary>What is Rampell's "distribution race" and how has AI changed its timeline?</summary>

"The battle of every startup versus incumbent is whether the startup gets the distribution before the incumbent gets the innovation." VisiCalc lost to Lotus 1-2-3 in ~5 years. Lotus lost to Excel in ~15 years. In 2025, this cycle can take weeks — every layer of past innovation (cloud, mobile, AI) compresses the time to build and distribute a competitive product. But systems of record remain sticky: data gravity persists even when software creation is cheap.
