<summary>What are the five tentacles of Tim Urban's Yearning Octopus, and why does naming them matter for career decisions?</summary>

Personal (fulfillment, purpose), Social (acceptance, admiration, approval), Lifestyle (comfort, freedom, predictability), Moral (impact, helping others), and Practical (paying the bills). Naming them matters because career decisions are the net output of whichever tentacles scream loudest — and without mapping them explicitly, you'll never notice which one is actually driving you, or that the tentacles are often fighting each other.
