<summary>Why does Tristan de Montebello call speaking a "meta skill" rather than a specialized skill?</summary>

Like fitness, improving your speaking sends ripple effects through everything else — work, friendships, confidence. The hardware (voice, expression, gesture) is already world-class from evolution. The problem is always software bugs: self-consciousness, overthinking, fear of judgment. Fix the software and the hardware performs.
