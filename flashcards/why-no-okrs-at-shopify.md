<summary>Why does Shopify have no OKRs, and what does Tobi Lütke use instead?</summary>

Goodhart's law: any metric that becomes a goal ceases to be useful. The most valuable product work is only ~20% quantifiable. Shopify invests heavily in data dashboards but treats them as cockpit instruments, not autopilot. The pilot (taste, craft, judgment) makes the decisions; the instruments inform them.

See: [Goodhart's Law in Product](../ideas/goodharts-law-in-product.md)

</details>
