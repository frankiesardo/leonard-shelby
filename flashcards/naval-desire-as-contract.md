<summary>Naval says "desire is a contract." A contract to do what? And what's the practical remedy?</summary>

A contract you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want. Every desire is an axis of suffering. The remedy is not to eliminate desire entirely — you're a biological creature with a mission — but to pick one overwhelming desire and consciously let the rest go. Most desires are picked up unconsciously and carried without examination.

See: [The Backwards Law](../ideas/the-backwards-law.md), [Guard the Door of Your Mind](../ideas/guard-the-door-of-your-mind.md)
