**Q:** Why does Manson argue that "everything being fucked doesn't require hope -- hope requires everything being fucked"?

**A:** Hope depends on the rejection of what currently is. It requires something to be broken so there is something to fix. The sources of hope that give life meaning are the same sources of division and conflict -- tribes form around shared hopes and define themselves against those who threaten them. The conflict maintains the hope. Therefore, hope is not just the response to a broken world; it is the mechanism that keeps the world feeling broken, even as material conditions improve.
