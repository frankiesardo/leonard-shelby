<summary>Why does Shopify build so much software internally instead of using commercial tools?</summary>

Every piece of software carries the worldview of its creators. Adopting Workday means adopting Workday's vision of HR. If your ideas differ from what's available, off-the-shelf software silently constrains your decisions. Building is expensive, but adopting the wrong worldview is more expensive.

See: [Software Has a Worldview](../ideas/software-has-a-worldview.md)
