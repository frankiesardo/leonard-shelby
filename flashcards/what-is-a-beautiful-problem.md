<details>
<summary>What does Tobi Lütke mean by "a beautiful problem" (via Karl Popper)?</summary>

A problem so deep you can never fully solve it, and even if you do, it has "plenty of enlightened problem children" — sub-problems that keep multiplying. This makes going vertical always more rewarding than going horizontal. Commerce is Tobi's beautiful problem; GPUs are Jensen Huang's.

See: [The Beautiful Problem](../ideas/the-beautiful-problem.md)
