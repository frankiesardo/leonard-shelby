<summary>How do you build and condense a peak-state trigger?</summary>

First, find an activity that reliably puts you in a state of serene focus. Build a multi-step routine around it (e.g., eat, exercise, meditate, listen to music). Practice the routine daily until the physiological link between routine and state is automatic. Then incrementally shorten it — fifteen minutes becomes twelve, becomes eight, becomes a single deep breath. The zone stops being something you hope to stumble into and becomes something you can summon.
