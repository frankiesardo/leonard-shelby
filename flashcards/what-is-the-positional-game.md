<summary>What is the "positional game" vs. the "tactical game" in business (Tobi Lütke's chess metaphor)?</summary>

Tactics (A/B tests, growth hacks) extract value from your position. Position (territory, trust, product depth) is what compounds. Companies that faded were "tacticked out of their position" — they optimized short-term gains until the foundation was depleted. The founder's job: play the positional game and create finite winnable games for the team.
