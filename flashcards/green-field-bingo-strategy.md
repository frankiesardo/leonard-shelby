<summary>What is "green field bingo" and when does it fail?</summary>

Alex Rampell's name for the strategy of selling to new companies rather than fighting incumbents for their hostages. Stripe didn't steal from Chase Paymentech — they sold to companies that didn't exist yet. It works when the rate of new entity creation in the target market is high enough (startups, SMBs, new legal cases). It fails when new entity creation is too slow — e.g., building a better EHR doesn't work because new hospitals aren't created fast enough.
