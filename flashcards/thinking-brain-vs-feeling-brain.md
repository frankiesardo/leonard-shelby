<details>
<summary>In Manson's "Consciousness Car" metaphor, which brain is driving and which is navigating -- and what evidence proves this?</summary>

The Feeling Brain drives; the Thinking Brain navigates. Damasio's patient "Elliot," after losing emotional capacity from a brain tumor, retained perfect intelligence but could not make decisions or function -- proving that emotions, not reason, are the engine of all action. Self-control is the illusion that occurs when both brains are aligned, not when reason overrides emotion.
