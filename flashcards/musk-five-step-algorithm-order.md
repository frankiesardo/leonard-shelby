<summary>What are Musk's five steps for process improvement, and why does the order matter?</summary>

1. Make requirements less dumb
2. Delete the part or process step
3. Simplify or optimize
4. Accelerate cycle time
5. Automate

The order is the point. Most engineers default to step 3, 4, or 5 — optimizing, accelerating, automating — without first questioning whether the thing should exist. Optimizing something that should be deleted is pure waste that looks like progress.

See: [Delete Before Optimizing](../ideas/delete-before-optimizing.md)
