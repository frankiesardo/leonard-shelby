<summary>What does the glass mat story illustrate about the danger of running the five steps in reverse?</summary>

Five glass mats on the Model 3 battery pack were choking the production line. Musk's mistake sequence: first automated the installation, then accelerated it, then optimized it — steps 5, 4, 3 — before anyone asked why the mats existed. When asked, the safety team said fire protection; the NVH team said noise and vibration. Neither was correct. The mats were deleted, and a $2 million automation robot became unnecessary overnight.

Running the steps backwards wastes not just time but capital. Automation locks in the wrong thing expensively.
