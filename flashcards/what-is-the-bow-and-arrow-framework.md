<details>
<summary>What is the "bow and arrow" framework for talks?</summary>

Shift from "what do I want to say" to "what do I want people to remember." The answer — one sentence — is the arrow. Everything else (stories, data, anecdotes) is the bow that gives it weight. Works at both macro (whole talk) and micro (each slide) levels. If a section doesn't have an arrow, it shouldn't exist.

See: [The Bow and Arrow](../ideas/the-bow-and-arrow.md)

</details>
