<summary>What does "EV > TV > MV" mean and when does it break down?</summary>

Enterprise Value > Team Value > My Value. Always optimize for the whole company first, then your team, then yourself. It breaks down at scale when departments form and managers start optimizing for their own metrics (TV) at the expense of the company (EV) — e.g., sales maximizing bookings while creating downstream problems for service. The fix requires structural mechanisms: explicit scoring in reviews, public feedback, and employee NPS by department to make the problem visible.

See: [Solve for Enterprise Value](../ideas/solve-for-enterprise-value.md)
