<summary>What three elements does a truthseeking group's charter need?</summary>

1. **Accuracy focus** — we're here to get closer to truth, not to feel good
2. **Accountability** — we'll call each other out and accept being called out
3. **Diversity of viewpoints** — homogeneous groups amplify bias rather than correcting it

Without all three, a group devolves into an echo chamber. Erik Seidel's three-sentence poker charter is the gold standard: "I don't want to hear bad luck stories. If you have a question about strategy, ask me all day. I don't see the purpose of a story about something outside your control."
