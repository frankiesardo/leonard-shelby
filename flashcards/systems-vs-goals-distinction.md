<summary>If winners and losers share the same goals, what actually explains the difference?</summary>

Their systems. Every Olympic athlete wants gold -- the goal is identical. What separates winners from losers is the system of continuous small improvements they follow daily. Goals set direction; systems produce results. "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."
