<summary>Why does Tobi Lütke argue that vertical depth beats horizontal expansion?</summary>

Depth compounds: understanding a domain deeply means the surface area of possibility keeps expanding. NVIDIA spent a career on GPUs rather than diversifying and became the most valuable company in the world. Business books advise going wide because most companies' core competency was having a stopwatch — that's commoditized. If your competency is domain understanding, depth wins.
