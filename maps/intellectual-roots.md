## Ongoing sources

Not books — living streams of ideas I draw from regularly.

- **Lenny's Podcast** — product, growth, leadership. Occasional deep thinkers surface here and get promoted into the garden.
- **Jordan Peterson's talks** — beyond the books, the lectures and conversations are a rich source of ideas on meaning, myth, and responsibility.
- **Paul Graham's essays** — "Build Something People Want," "Founder Mode," and many others. Short, opinionated, deeply practical.
- **Harari's other books** — Homo Deus, 21 Lessons for the 21st Century. The Sapiens worldview extended.
